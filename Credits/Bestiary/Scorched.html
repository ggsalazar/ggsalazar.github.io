Small motes of hovering light that from a distance appear harmless, but upon close inspection are actually autonomous balls of tightly-knit streaks of lightning emanating from a small central mass.

Passive Abilities
Proximity Shock. All creatures standing within 5 feet of the Lightning Wisp take 3d6 electric damage at the start of each of their turns and when they get close enough to the Wisp (removing a single reaction if this damage was dealt before the start of the targeted creature's turn); this damage is reduced only by the creature's Armor score; this damage is also dealt with advantage and is final if a creature touches it with a conductive object.
Actions
Ignimancy. The Lightning Wisp has 6 Mana that it can use to cast lightning-based Ignimancy spells; it uses a d6 for its Aether Die.
Headbutt. Melee attack, 1 target, 1/action, 1d4 physical/electric damage.
Tactics
Lightning Wisps prefer to travel and attack in groups of 3+1d3, and when attacking, try to come in at their prey from all sides or from each exit to a given area, knowing that their Proximity Shock ability will almost certainly eliminate any hope their target has of fleeing.

Aether: Fire Aether, 1/2 lb.
